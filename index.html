HOW BACKROOM WORKS

  • 📍 Location: Set a city once. It stays active across Search Results, Venues and Events.
  • 🔎 Search Results: Type a venue or party name to search both venues and event names.
  • 🏛️ Venues: Browse every venue in your selected city.
  • 📅 Events: Browse what is on by date, city, or vibe tags.
  • 💖 My Events: Save events you plan to attend.
  • ⚜️ Favourites: Keep your own go-to venues.
  • 📑 Shortlists: Build a shareable mix of venues and events, then send it to someone else to plan a night out.
